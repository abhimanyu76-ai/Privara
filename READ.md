# Privara — Privacy & AI Governance Suite
A full-stack Privacy and AI Governance platform developed as part of PwC Middle East's Digital Trust, Cyber & Privacy practice.
## Modules
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (GDPR Art.35 & UAE PDPL)
- AI Governance (NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, UAE AI Strategy)
- AI Security Governance (OWASP LLM Top 10, MITRE ATLAS)
- Incident & Breach Management
- Unified Control Library
- AI Policy Advisor
